# Privacy Policy

**CLI Pulse**
**Last Updated: March 28, 2026**

## Overview

CLI Pulse ("the App") is a developer tool that monitors usage across cloud CLI providers (AWS, GCP, Azure, etc.). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (used for authentication and account identification)
- Device name and platform (used for multi-device sync)

### Usage Data
- Cloud CLI provider usage metrics (API call counts, token usage, cost estimates)
- Provider configuration preferences (which providers are enabled)
- App settings and preferences

### Subscription Information
- Subscription tier and status (managed by Apple via StoreKit)
- We do not directly collect or store payment information — all transactions are processed by Apple

## How We Use Your Information

- **Service delivery**: To display your CLI usage metrics and alerts
- **Multi-device sync**: To synchronize data across your registered devices
- **Notifications**: To send usage alerts and quota warnings (when enabled)
- **Subscription management**: To enforce tier-based feature limits

## Data Storage and Retention

- Usage data is stored on your self-hosted backend server
- Data retention periods depend on your subscription tier:
  - Free: 7 days
  - Pro: 90 days
  - Team: 365 days
- Data older than your retention period is automatically deleted

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your usage data remains on your own infrastructure.

## Third-Party Services

- **Apple StoreKit**: Processes in-app subscriptions. See [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/).
- **GitHub OAuth**: Used for authentication. See [GitHub's Privacy Policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).

## Your Rights

You can:
- View all data associated with your account within the app
- Delete your account and associated data by signing out and removing your backend data
- Disable notifications at any time
- Hide personal information (email) in the app UI

## Children's Privacy

CLI Pulse is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted to this page with an updated revision date.
