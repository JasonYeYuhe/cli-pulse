About

FAQ

How does CLI Pulse collect data?
A lightweight helper daemon runs on your Mac or server and detects AI tool processes. It periodically syncs usage snapshots to your private dashboard.

Is my data private?
Yes. Each account's data is isolated. We do not share or sell usage data. No source code, prompts, or conversation content is ever collected or transmitted.

Which platforms are supported?
iOS, macOS, and Home Screen widgets. The helper runs on macOS and Linux.

What are the subscription tiers?
Free tier includes 2 providers, 1 device, and 7-day data retention. Pro adds unlimited providers, 5 devices, 30-day retention, and cost tracking. Team adds collaboration features.

Privacy Policy

CLI Pulse collects only aggregated usage metrics (token counts, cost estimates, session durations) from locally detected AI tool processes. No source code, prompts, or conversation content is ever collected or transmitted. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings.
