Support

Get help

  • General support: clipulse.support@gmail.com
  • Bug reports & feature requests: github.com/JasonYeYuhe/cli-pulse/issues
  • Security disclosures: yyyyy.yeyuhe@gmail.com with subject [CLI Pulse Security] — see security.html first

Response time: within 48 hours on business days. CLI Pulse is built and supported by a single developer, so deeper investigations may take longer; reports with clear repro steps and screenshots are triaged fastest.

Before you file a bug

Including the following lets us reproduce the issue much faster:

  • Platform and version (e.g. macOS 15.3, iOS 18.4, Android 15)
  • CLI Pulse app version (Settings → About)
  • Which provider(s) are involved (Claude / Codex / Gemini / etc.)
  • Whether you're signed in and whether sync is on
  • Reproduction steps and expected vs. actual behavior
  • Screenshots if the issue is visible in the UI

Please do not paste API keys, session cookies, or full session-log JSONL contents into a bug report. We don't need them, and we explicitly ask not to receive them.

Subscriptions and billing

CLI Pulse subscriptions are billed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Billing). Manage, change, or cancel your subscription in:

  • iPhone / iPad / Mac: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions
  • Apple Watch: manage from your iPhone using the same path
  • Android: Google Play app → profile menu → Payments & subscriptions

Refund requests are handled by Apple or Google directly through the same store accounts. We can help with anything store-side that requires our confirmation — please email clipulse.support@gmail.com.

Account management

  • Sign out: Settings → Account → Sign Out
  • Delete account / data: Settings → Account → Delete Account — cascading deletes remove all associated rows within 30 days
  • Export your data: Overview tab → Export Report — produces a PDF or CSV

Privacy & security

  • Privacy Policy — what stays on your device, what syncs to your account, who the sub-processors are.
  • Data Handling — per-category table.
  • Security Policy — vulnerability reporting and credential handling.

Downloads

  • iOS / iPadOS / watchOS — App Store
  • macOS — signed & notarized DMG on GitHub Releases
  • Android (beta) — Google Play internal testing or APK on GitHub Releases

Binaries from anywhere not listed above are not authentic.
